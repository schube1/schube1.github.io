Meet Coach Vinzent

I'm Vinzent Schubert, a soccer coach with over 15 years of experience as a player and mentor. I currently play on the San Jose State University club team and have trained kids of all skill levels.

My Approach

My sessions focus on making the game fun, building confidence, and developing fundamentals. Every child progresses differently, so I personalize every session to their goals.

Coaching Values

  • Growth mindset
  • Discipline

My Philosophy
